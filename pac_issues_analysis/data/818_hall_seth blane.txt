A native of St. Elizabeth, Mo., in Miller County, Blaine Luetkemeyer is a lifelong resident of the district and knows what it takes to improve the economy and create jobs from his more than 30 years of experience as a small businessman.

Luetkemeyer was first elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2008 and was re-elected in 2010, 2012, 2014, and 2016. He has been endorsed by NFIB, Missouri Right to Life, and the Us Chamber for reelection in 2018.
